Installation

Follow the below steps to install Lost Lands in an Android device:

  1. Open Play Store.

  2. Enter Lost Lands in the search bar.

    A list of Lost Lands games is displayed.

  3. Click any one of the game

    Tip: It is recommended to play the game in the order of release version.

  4. Click Install.

  5. Click Open or search for the game in the list of installed applications.
